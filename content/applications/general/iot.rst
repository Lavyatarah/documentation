========================
Internet of Things (IoT)
========================

**Odoo IoT** is a bridge between the physical world and the digital world.
This application allows you to interact with certain devices from your Odoo instance.

   - `Odoo Tutorials: Internet of Things (IoT) Tutorials <https://www.odoo.com/slides/internet-of-things-iot-175>`_
